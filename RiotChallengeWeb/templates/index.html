FAQ

How will you know?

Our algorithm uses advanced Machine Learning techniques to predict your chances of winning based on your team's composition, Summoners' skill, runes, masteries, etc...

Why only NA?

For the purposes of this prototype due to time and API rate limit constraints we were only able to train the model on games from NA. Thus, to ensure best accuracy the prototype is restricted to NA only.

Does this work only for URF games?

While only URF game data was available for training the model, this prototype will still predict your chances of winning in a regular NA match. However, the most acculturate predictions will be made for URF games.

Summoner not found in NA live game...?

The Summoner you are searching for must be in a live game on NA servers for us to make a prediction.
